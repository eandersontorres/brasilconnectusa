## 8. Why Mercury specifically

```
Mercury is the natural fit for our audience for three reasons:

1. **Accepts foreign founders without SSN** — most US banks require SSN or ITIN before opening business accounts, which creates a 3-6 month delay for newly-arrived Brazilian founders. Mercury accepts EIN-only applications, which means a Brazilian who just formed a Delaware LLC can have a working US business account within a week. This is the single biggest unlock we point to in /guias/abrir-llc.

2. **API-first banking matches our audience's stack** — many of our users are running Stripe-paying SaaS, Shopify stores, or content businesses. Mercury's API + native Stripe/Shopify integrations remove a layer of operational pain that legacy banks impose. This is a real reason our users *stay* with Mercury after onboarding.

3. **No monthly fees + reasonable wire fees for international** — Brazilian founders eventually need to wire funds to BR (taxes, business expenses, investments). Mercury's pricing is transparent and competitive vs. legacy banks where wire fees can be $35-50 each. We surface this calculation explicitly.

We want Mercury to become the default banking recommendation for the Brazilian-American founder cohort.
```
