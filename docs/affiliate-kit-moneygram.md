## 8. Why MoneyGram specifically

```
MoneyGram is the natural fit for our audience for three reasons:

1. **Complementary to WU, not redundant** — many Brazilians have a single nearby cash pickup agent and it might be MoneyGram, not WU. By offering both we capture users who'd otherwise abandon /remessas saying "WU doesn't work for my mom's neighborhood." Real example: in interior cities of Pernambuco and Ceará, MoneyGram's network through smaller retail (mercados, drogarias) often beats WU.

2. **$0 first-send promos are consistent** — MoneyGram runs near-permanent "$0 fee on first send" campaigns through their app. We position these promos prominently in /guia-chegada because the first-transfer moment is when new immigrants are most price-sensitive.

3. **Mobile app UX has improved dramatically** — the 2024-2025 MoneyGram app overhaul means our users get a workable mobile-first experience, which matters for a 25-45 year old audience who never opens desktop banking. We can confidently recommend the app, not just the website.

We want to be a long-term partner and contribute to MoneyGram's growing share in the Brazil corridor.
```
