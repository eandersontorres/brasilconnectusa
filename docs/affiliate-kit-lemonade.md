## 5. Audience description

```
Audience: Brazilians living in the United States (1st-generation immigrants, students, professionals on H1B/L1, dual citizens, undocumented workers).

Geographic concentration: Massachusetts, Florida, Texas, California, New Jersey, Georgia.

Language: 95% Portuguese-only or Portuguese-preferred.

Lemonade-fit segments:
- **Renters insurance:** ~40% of monthly users are renters (apartments, not homeowners) — most have never carried renters insurance in Brazil and don't know it's normal/required in the US. We educate + convert.
```

## 8. Why Lemonade specifically

```
Lemonade is the natural fit for our audience for three reasons:

1. **90-second app-based signup is the only flow that works for our audience** — Brazilians without long US credit histories or English fluency get rejected or quoted absurdly by Geico/Progressive/State Farm. Lemonade's algorithmic underwriting + chatbot UI removes the language friction and accepts thin-credit profiles. We've seen real users sign up for Renters in under 5 minutes from their phone.

2. **Renters insurance is genuinely under-penetrated in the Brazilian immigrant population** — most Brazilians arrived from a country where renters don't carry insurance, and many landlords here don't enforce the requirement. Lemonade's $5-15/month price point makes this an easy "yes" once we educate. Conversion on /guia-chegada is materially higher than the comparator-style competitors.

3. **Charitable giveback aligns with our audience values** — Lemonade's Giveback program (unused premium donated to charities of customer's choice) resonates strongly with Brazilian community values around solidarity. We surface this in Portuguese and it differentiates Lemonade vs. nameless legacy insurers our audience already distrusts.

We want to deepen the existing Lemonade integration as our user base grows.
```
